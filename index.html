Social Policy Expert | Human Capital, Social Protection, Employment Policy, Governance

Mariami Paposhvili is a policy practitioner and researcher with over ten years of experience advancing social and economic equity through labour and social policy reform. Currently based at Expertise France in Tbilisi, working on Human Capital Development with a focus on employment, labour rights, disability inclusion, social-services delivery, and cybersecurity policy reforms.

She holds an MA in International Policy from Texas A&M University (USA) and an MA in European Interdisciplinary Studies from the College of Europe (Poland).
