Mariami Paposhvili is a policy expert and researcher specializing in international development, social policy, and Eastern Partnership relations. With extensive experience in project management, research, and policy analysis, she has worked with leading international organizations including Expertise France, Open Society Foundations, and the European Parliament.

Her work focuses on social justice, decent work policies, democratic resilience, and human rights across the Eastern Partnership region. She holds a Master's in International Policy from Texas A&M University and a Master's in European Interdisciplinary Studies from the College of Europe.
